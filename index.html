Our Results for Children in Bangladesh

Your support shows that lasting, large-scale change is possible. Last year, we changed the lives of over 2,623,000 children in Bangladesh. Together,
we’re giving them the opportunities every child deserves
– to grow up healthy, learning and safe.

* 2,050,000 children healthy and nourished

* 286,000 children educated and empowered

* 306,000 children protected from harm

* 277,000 children lifted from poverty

* 274,000 children aided in crisis
